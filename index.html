About Us

Who We Are

We are the Youth Wing of the All Progressives Congress (APC), Africa’s largest political organization and Nigeria’s ruling party with a membership strength of over 40 million. Our membership is open to all young Nigerians, home and abroad, between the ages of 18 and 40.
We operate as an autonomous body within the overall structure of the party and maintain an active presence in the entire country and beyond through a National, Zonal, Senatorial, State, LGA, Ward, and Unit structure led by respective leaders who manage and supervise the implementation of party goals and objectives as determined and approved by the National Youth Leader, who heads the wing and is a member of the National Working Committee (NWC), the party’s highest leadership organ.

Our primary obligation is the promotion of the party and the broadening of its acceptance among young Nigerians through an abiding fidelity to our progressive values and ideals, including generational balance and equitable representation, and the implementation of ideas and initiatives that demonstrate the party’s commitment to modern and transformational leadership that leverages and pushes social and technological advancements.
As part of the efforts to achieve this, including the organization of the Wing into a distinct and verifiable structure necessary for effective mobilization and engagement, the Dayo Israel-led Youth Wing commissioned the country’s first-of-its-kind dedicated youth membership registration exercise that allows interested young Nigerians across the country and beyond to formally register with the party and instantly obtain proof of their membership in a modern, efficient, and convenient process.
